CONTENT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM PLATFORMS

A Content Management System (CMS) platform helps users to create, manage, and publish content, media, and documents. This platform allows you to manage your content better and publish website content. Companies use CMS platforms as a document management system, an intranet site, and to publish web content on their blogs and websites.
